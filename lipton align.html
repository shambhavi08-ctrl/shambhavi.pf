LIPTON

The Lipton Green Tea campaign redesign focuses on refreshing the brand's identity with a modern and natural aesthetic. The logo features a single, elegant green tea leaf that curves around the bottom portion of a sun disc, symbolizing the natural ingredients and the refreshing, rejuvenating nature of the product. The sun, with its bright rays against a yellow-orange gradient circle, conveys positivity, energy, and warmth, creating a friendly and approachable vibe. The color palette incorporates shades of green to represent freshness, while the vibrant yellow-orange gradient adds vitality. This redesign is applied across various touchpoints, including packaging, billboards, and posters, all of which reinforce the message of natural refreshment and appeal to health-conscious consumers. The overall concept aims to create a visual identity that feels both modern and welcoming, aligning with Lipton's mission of providing a refreshing, nature-inspired experience.
