About Me

I’m currently pursuing my B-des degree in Communication Design from Atlasskilltech University Mumbai. I have a passion for creating designs that tell compelling stories and connect with people. My journey in design started with a deep interest in visual arts and grew into a desire to craft meaningful solutions for brands and individuals. Through my academic assignments and observership, I’ve had the opportunity to work with a diverse range of projects, developing everything from brand identities to packaging and digital designs. I believe in the importance of collaboration and always strive to stay curious and open to new ideas, which helps me evolve as a designer. When I’m not designing, you’ll find me exploring new artistic mediums, keeping up with design trends, or simply enjoying the beauty of everyday life.

Design is not just about aesthetics; it’s about creating meaningful connections. Every design decision is a step towards telling a story, solving a problem, and engaging an audience. I aim to keep pushing the boundaries of creativity, blending art and functionality in every project I work on.
